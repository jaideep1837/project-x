1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Skilltrade Ltd (“we,” “our,” or “us”), a company incorporated in Mauritius, collects, uses, discloses, and protects your personal information when you interact with our services.

We comply with the Mauritius Data Protection Act 2017, which incorporates principles similar to the EU GDPR.

2. Personal Information We Collect

2.1 Directly from You

Information provided when registering for a challenge, completing verification (KYC), making payments, or contacting our support team.

2.2 From Third Parties

We may receive data from financial service providers, payment processors, or regulatory authorities, as required by law.

2.3 Automatically Collected

Cookies, IP addresses, and device/browser information to enhance security and improve your experience.

3. Sensitive Information

We do not collect sensitive personal data unless strictly necessary and with your explicit consent, or where required by Mauritian law.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • Providing access to trading evaluations and funded accounts
  • Processing transactions and verifying identity
  • Fraud prevention and regulatory compliance
  • Customer support and communication
  • System security, audits, and improvements
  • Marketing (where permitted)

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and tracking technologies to improve performance and user experience. You can disable cookies in your browser, but this may limit functionality.

6. Sharing Your Information

We may share your personal information with:

  • Service providers (IT, payment processors, hosting, compliance)
  • Regulators, auditors, and government agencies in Mauritius or abroad where legally required
  • Business partners in case of mergers, acquisitions, or restructuring

Any cross-border transfer of data will comply with Mauritian data protection law.

7. Your Rights

Under the Mauritius Data Protection Act 2017, you have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Correct or update your data
  • Request deletion (“right to be forgotten”)
  • Restrict or object to certain processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time

You may also file a complaint with the Data Protection Office of Mauritius.

8. Data Security & Retention

We apply encryption, secure servers, and restricted access measures. Data is stored only as long as necessary to fulfill legal and contractual obligations, unless a longer retention period is required by law.
